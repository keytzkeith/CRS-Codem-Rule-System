CRS Codem System Rule trading journal

CRS is a personal trading journal focused on rule-based review, import workflows, and practical performance analysis. Record trades, inspect execution quality, and keep your process tight without the clutter of a broad social platform.

Trading journal workflow with CSV import

CRS supports manual logging and CSV import for broker-exported trades. Review entries, exits, notes, setup stacks, and rule validation in one focused workspace built around your own process.

Execution review and performance analytics

Use the dashboard, analytics views, and calendar review to inspect net P&L, R-multiples, streaks, session behavior, and trade quality over time. The goal is practical review, not product sprawl.

Self-hosted and under your control

CRS can be developed locally and later deployed to your own live environment. The current build supports a practical self-hosted workflow with local Postgres, Docker-assisted services, and CRS-native import/export contracts.

Focused on your own system

CRS is designed around personal rule tracking, journal discipline, and execution review. It is intentionally narrower than the broader legacy codebase and keeps the active product focused on your workflow.
